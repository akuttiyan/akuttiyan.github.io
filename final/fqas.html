Porcelain and ceramic tiles are popular choices for kitchens and bathrooms due to their water resistance and durability. For outdoor spaces, consider porcelain or natural stone tiles that can withstand varying weather conditions.

Ceramic tiles are made from clay and are suitable for indoor use. Porcelain tiles are denser and more durable than ceramic tiles, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and outdoor use. Natural stone tiles, like marble or granite, offer unique variations and natural beauty.
